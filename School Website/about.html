About Us


Saurabh Shaikshnik Samajik Sansthan aims to be an outstanding school by offering a broad and balanced curriculum which evolves to reflect the needs of the pupil and needs of modern society.

With the state of art infrastructure and personal care of each child, the school strives to develop a positive ethos and learning culture in every pupil.

ICSE – Comprehensive and structured curriculum of ICSE acts as an imperative pillar to provide more practical knowledge to students and helps to build analytical skills in them.

Our Vision

To educate all students to the highest levels of academic achievement, to enable them to reach and expand their potential, and to prepare them to become productive, responsible, ethical, creative and compassionate members of society.



Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide high quality education and childcare in a safe, vibrant and positive environment that builds a foundation for life-long learning.

Message from the Principal

In today’s pursuit of holistic education, our S S Shettar Foundation’s, Veda International School has emerged as key influencers in shaping a child’s overall development. Holistic education, acknowledging a student’s emotional, physical, social, and spiritual growth, is a central tenet of our school. This approach, in line with the New Education Policy of 2020 in India, emphasizes a blend of academics with social responsibilities and humanitarian values, laying the groundwork for a more balanced academic and social upbringing. Our school relatively novel in the Indian educational landscape standout due to our distinctive teaching methodologies and curriculum design, tailored to address a child’s emotional and physical needs comprehensively with highly equipped state of the art infrastructure, modern technologies, teacher’s training levels, sports activities, public speaking, inter school competitions, well equipped laboratories, library, transportation, medical aids, music, dance, etc. We urge you all to make the toughest question easy which any parent faces initially is the question of a good school which stands out to bring the best from the students and experience the best quality education which every child should experience.
