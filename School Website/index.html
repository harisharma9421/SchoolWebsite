About Us

Chetana Vikas Mulya Shiksha School of education aims to be an outstanding school by offering a broad and balanced curriculum which evolves to reflect the needs of the pupil and needs of modern society with the state of art infrastructure and personal care of each child, the school strives to develop a positive ethos and learning culture in every pupil. ICSE – Comprehensive and structured curriculum of ICSE acts as an imperative pillar to provide more practical knowledge to students and helps to build analytical skills in them.
